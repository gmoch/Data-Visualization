Visualizations of pass rates, race, and gender for 2013 in the US

By Will Murphy and Granger Moch

Tuesday, November 11th 2014



*Only states for which enough data was available appear in the datasets that these graph reflect. Because the College Board doesn't report scores if the total in a category is less than 5, we don't know how many students passed if there are less than 5 people in a category. The states whose test scores matched that criterion were not included in these graphs. Moreover, the states in which no students took the test were not included either, since our purpose was to compare the scores of students who in fact took the test.
